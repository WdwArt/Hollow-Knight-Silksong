Pharloom by Region

Lower Regions

The lower regions of Pharloom serve as the starting areas for Hornet's journey. These areas are relatively safer and contain more settlements where pilgrims have gathered. The silk infestation is present but less overwhelming here.

Middle Regions

As Hornet ascends, the middle regions present greater challenges with more complex layouts, stronger enemies, and heavier silk infestation. These areas feature the iconic Team Cherry level design with interconnected paths and numerous shortcuts.

Upper Regions

The upper regions, including The Citadel, represent the pinnacle of Pharloom's religious and political power. These areas are heavily guarded, feature the most challenging platforming sections, and contain the game's most formidable bosses.

Hidden Areas

Scattered throughout Pharloom are numerous hidden areas, secret passages, and optional challenges that reward thorough exploration and skilled play. These areas often contain the best equipment and most powerful upgrades.

Exploration Tips

🗺️

Map Every Area

Purchase maps from Shakra and fill them completely to uncover all secrets and shortcuts.

🧵

Use Silk Abilities

Master Hornet's silk-based movement abilities to reach previously inaccessible areas.

👂

Listen for Audio Cues

Pay attention to environmental sounds and music changes that indicate nearby secrets.

🔍

Examine Walls

Breakable walls and hidden passages are common throughout Pharloom.

📋

Complete Quests

Many quests lead to new areas or provide keys to unlock previously inaccessible locations.

💎

Collect Everything

Gather resources and items that might be needed to craft tools for accessing new areas.
