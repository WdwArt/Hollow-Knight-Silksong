What is Hollow Knight: Silksong?

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed Hollow Knight, developed and published by Team Cherry. This epic action-adventure game follows Hornet, princess-protector of Hallownest, as she embarks on a perilous quest through a strange new kingdom.

Originally planned as downloadable content for Hollow Knight, the project grew in scope to become a full standalone sequel, offering players a vast new world to explore with expanded gameplay mechanics and an all-new protagonist.

Meet Team Cherry

Team Cherry is an independent game development studio based in Adelaide, Australia. Founded by Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and David Kazi, the team gained worldwide recognition with their debut title Hollow Knight.

With Silksong, Team Cherry has expanded their vision while maintaining the meticulous attention to detail and passion that made their first game a masterpiece. The studio's commitment to quality is evident in every aspect of Silksong's design.
