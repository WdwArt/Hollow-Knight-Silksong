Boss Strategies

The Weaver

Location: Silken Weald

Focus on dodging silk attacks and striking during cooldown periods. Use your own silk abilities to counter her thread manipulation.

Pattern-based Silk Counter

Citadel Guardian

Location: The Citadel

Master parrying and timing for this heavily armored foe. Target weak points and use mobility to avoid devastating attacks.

Parry Focus Weak Points

Flame Warden

Location: Furnace Depths

Environmental awareness is key. Use terrain to your advantage and maintain constant pressure to prevent fire buildup.

Environmental Pressure

Frost Queen

Location: Storm Peaks

Break ice formations and exploit thaw moments. Mobility is crucial to avoid freezing effects and area-denial attacks.

Elemental Mobility
