Boss Strategies

The Weaver

Location: Silken Weald

Focus on dodging silk attacks and striking during cooldown periods. Use your own silk abilities to counter her thread manipulation.

Pattern-based Silk Counter

Citadel Guardian

Location: The Citadel

Master parrying and timing for this heavily armored foe. Target weak points and use mobility to avoid devastating attacks.

Parry Focus Weak Points

Flame Warden

Location: Furnace Depths

Environmental awareness is key. Use terrain to your advantage and maintain constant pressure to prevent fire buildup.

Environmental Pressure

Frost Queen

Location: Storm Peaks

Break ice formations and exploit thaw moments. Mobility is crucial to avoid freezing effects and area-denial attacks.

Elemental Mobility

Essential Tips

🗺️

Map Everything

Purchase and fill maps completely to uncover all secrets and shortcuts throughout Pharloom.

🧵

Master Silk

Silk abilities are essential for both combat and exploration. Practice all silk techniques regularly.

💰

Manage Resources

Balance spending Rosaries on upgrades vs. saving for potential death losses.

📋

Complete Quests

Quests provide valuable rewards and often unlock new areas or abilities.

🔍

Explore Thoroughly

Secret passages and hidden areas are common. Break suspicious walls and check corners.

⚡

Stay Mobile

Hornet's strength is agility. Keep moving during combat to avoid damage.
