Protagonist

Hornet

Princess-Protector of Hallownest

Hornet is the daughter of the Pale King and Herrah the Beast, making her a unique hybrid of wyrm and spider. As the princess-protector of Hallownest, she has dedicated her life to protecting the kingdom and its inhabitants. After being kidnapped and transported to Pharloom, she must uncover the truth behind her capture while navigating this strange new kingdom.
